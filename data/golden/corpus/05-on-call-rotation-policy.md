# On-Call Rotation Policy

## Rotation Schedule

On-call rotates weekly, with handoff every **Monday at 10:00 AM Pacific**. Each week has one primary and one secondary on-call engineer.

## Who Is On Call

Use **Roster**, Northwind's internal lookup tool, to see who is currently primary and secondary on call, and who is scheduled for future weeks.

## Compensation & Time Off

On-call engineers receive a **$200 stipend** per week of on-call duty. Any page received overnight (between 10:00 PM and 6:00 AM local time) earns the engineer one comp day, in addition to the stipend.

## Escalation Contacts

If the primary on-call engineer does not respond to a page within **15 minutes**, the secondary on-call engineer listed in Roster is paged next.

## Swapping Shifts

On-call shifts may be swapped with another engineer through Roster. Swap requests must be submitted at least **48 hours in advance**, except in genuine emergencies, which the on-call manager may approve on shorter notice.
